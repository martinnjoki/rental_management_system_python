About Mbungu Properties

Professional rental operations built around landlords and tenants in Kenya.

We help you run rentals with less paperwork and clearer visibility into rent, occupancy, and arrears.

Mbungu Properties Management Limited delivers structured tenant records, payment tracking, and vacancy awareness so owners can focus on decisions—not spreadsheets.

Our aim is straightforward: dependable processes and modern tools that keep your portfolio organized and your cash flow predictable.

Why choose us

  • Tenant management — onboarding, contacts, and unit linkage in one place.
  • Rent tracking — record payments and methods with a clear monthly view.
  • Arrears visibility — see who is behind relative to expected rent.
  • Professional support — aligned with how Kenyan landlords and agents work.

Our Services

Practical tools for listings, rent, and tenant operations—matched to how this platform works today.

Property & units

Register properties and units, set rent amounts, and see which homes are vacant or occupied.

Rent & receipts

Record payments with dates and methods (including M-Pesa codes), download receipts, and review history.

Tenants & arrears

Maintain tenant profiles tied to units and spot arrears against expected monthly rent at a glance.
